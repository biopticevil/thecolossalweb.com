Did you know!

did you know that the colossal cumputer was actually used in WWII by the brittish? not every body does. It was made by Tommy Flowers. who is that? Thomas Harold Flowers, BSc, DSc, MBE was an English engineer with the British General Post Office. During World War II, Flowers designed and built Colossus, the world's first programmable electronic computer, to help solve encrypted German messages.
